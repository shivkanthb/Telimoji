What is CardioTend?

CardioTend is a real time heart monitoring system, which helps patients to detect various heart related diseases or abnormalities like PVC, AFib, etc. CardioTend builds a bridge between patients and doctors, and provides a remote monitor to doctors.

How dangerous is Atrial Fibrillation?

It is possible to have AFib, the most common type of irregular heartbeat without having any symptoms and to be unaware of your condition until a doctor discovers it during a physical exam. A standard EKG only records the heartbeat for a few seconds making it hard to detect especially during tests. In Europe and North America, as of 2014, it affects about 2% to 3% of the population.
